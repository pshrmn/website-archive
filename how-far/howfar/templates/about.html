Why?

People can perform some pretty crazy athletic feats. Martin Strel swam the entire length of the Mississippi River. Cliff Young won a 544 mile ultramarathon across Australia at age 61, covering the distance in less than six days. While very few people can match these endeavors, How Far? provides a way to set your own long distance goals and achieve them over a more reasonable time frame.

How are distances measured?

Distances are measured as the crow flies based on the coordinates of each city. The measurements are calculated using the Haversine formula
